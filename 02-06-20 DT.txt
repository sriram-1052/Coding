n,k=map(int,input().split())
a=list(input().split())[:n]
b=[]
for i in a:
    c=[]
    for j in i:
        c.append(j.lower())
    b.append(c)
g=0
for i in range(len(b)-1):
    h=1
    for j in range(i+1,len(b)):
        if(b[i]==b[j]):
            h+=1
    if(h>g):
        g=h
if(g>=k):
    print("yes")
else:
    print("no")


Output:-
Input:
5 3
apple orange orange orange Banana

Output:
yes

Explanation:
Here N = 5 and K = 3.
In the given 5 string values, the string orange occurred 3 times consecutively. So yes is printed as the output.
